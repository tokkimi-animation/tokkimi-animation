# Guide des prompts visuels IA

Les prompts peuvent rester en anglais pour obtenir de meilleurs résultats, mais toutes les consignes de production sont expliquées ici en français.

## Bloc de style commun

Ajouter ce bloc à la fin de chaque prompt :

```text
gentle Korean preschool animation, soft watercolor and clean rounded line art,
pastel cream lavender moon-yellow sky-blue palette, cozy moon world,
simple readable silhouettes, soft diffuse lighting, child-safe atmosphere,
16:9 composition, consistent character proportions, no text, no logo
```

## Bloc fixe de Luni

```text
Luni, a small cream-white moon rabbit, one long ear and one softly folded ear,
a golden crescent ornament on the left ear, large warm brown eyes,
pink cheeks, navy-blue short overalls with a tiny gold star pocket,
white short-sleeve shirt, rounded childlike proportions
```

## Blocs des amis

```text
Byeori: small five-point pastel-yellow star, warm brown eyes, tiny arms and legs
Mongi: small white mochi-shaped cloud, tiny sky-blue bowler hat, soft blue raindrops
Kongkong: caramel squirrel, large curled tail, green scarf, brown acorn satchel
Moon Grandma: round elderly moon guardian, circular glasses, purple star-pattern robe
and nightcap, golden star wand
```

## Formule d’un prompt

```text
[bloc fixe du personnage],
[décor et moment de la journée],
[une seule action principale],
[émotion et expression],
[type de plan et angle de caméra],
[bloc de style commun]
```

## Exemple

```text
Luni, a small cream-white moon rabbit, one long ear and one softly folded ear,
a golden crescent ornament on the left ear, navy-blue short overalls,
standing beside Byeori at the glowing Star Lake at night,
Luni carefully reaches toward a tiny lost star reflected on the water,
curious and reassuring expression, medium wide shot at child eye level,
gentle Korean preschool animation, soft watercolor and clean rounded line art,
pastel cream lavender moon-yellow sky-blue palette, 16:9, no text, no logo
```

## Prompts de décors

### Village de la Lune

```text
small pastel houses with crescent windows, winding lavender paths,
warm yellow lamps, soft clouds below the village, deep blue starry sky,
welcoming central square, wide establishing shot
```

### Forêt des Nuages

```text
rounded mint and sky-blue trees growing from fluffy cloud ground,
soft floating leaves, safe winding path, distant moonlight,
dreamy but bright preschool fantasy environment
```

### Lac des Étoiles

```text
calm indigo lake reflecting stars and crescent moon,
rounded dark-blue trees, tiny glowing flowers along the shore,
gentle ripples and warm lantern light
```

### Colline Arc-en-ciel

```text
rolling mint hills, pastel flowers, large soft rainbow,
cream clouds and small golden stars, sunny diffuse light,
open playful landscape
```

## Éléments à exclure

```text
photorealistic, 3D plastic texture, horror, sharp teeth, weapons,
dark threatening shadows, cluttered composition, extra limbs,
different outfit, missing crescent ornament, distorted face,
written text, watermark, logo
```

## Contrôle de cohérence

1. Utiliser la planche officielle comme référence d’image.
2. Conserver exactement les mêmes blocs de description.
3. Ne demander qu’une scène et une action à la fois.
4. Réutiliser une image de face validée comme référence.
5. Vérifier les oreilles, le croissant, les vêtements, les yeux et les proportions.
